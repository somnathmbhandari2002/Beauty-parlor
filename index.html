FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries about booking, styling, and services.

We recommend booking bridal packages at least 3 to 6 months in advance. This ensures availability of your preferred senior stylist, slots for your trial makeup session, and coordinate scheduling for your bridal party.

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellation or rescheduling. For luxury suites and packages exceeding $200, we ask for 48 hours notice. Cancellations made with less notice may incur a 50% reservation fee.

We use exclusively high-end, cruelty-free, and organic-certified lines, including Oway Organic Haircare, Kerastase luxury lines, and Biologique Recherche for customized skincare treatments.

Yes, absolutely. We offer 3 premium glassmorphic VIP private suites. Booking a suite is complimentary with our Royal Package and Bridal Package, and can be added to standard bookings for a nominal fee of $30.

While we prioritize reserved appointments to guarantee a calm, zero-wait environment, we do accept walk-ins based on hourly availability. We recommend calling our priority VIP line before dropping by.
